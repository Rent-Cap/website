# About Us

Tenantprotections.org is a collaborative project among Code for San Francisco volunteers, TechEquity Collaborative, Community Legal Services in East Palo Alto (CLESPA), the Alliance of Californians for Community Empowerment (ACCE), and a coalition of partners who helped co-sponsor the Tenant Protection Act of 2019 (AB 1482).

## Our Mission

This site exists to provide renters in California with high quality information about their rights under both California law, and local ordinances. We connect renters with local and state-wide non-profits to help them deal with housing issues in their communities.

## About Our Partner Organizations

- [Alliance of Californians for Community Empowerment (ACCE)](https://www.acceaction.org/) - ACCE were a co-sponsor Tenant Protection Act of 2019 (AB 1482).
- [Code for San Francisco](https://codeforsanfrancisco.org/) - CfSF is a Code for America Bridage empowering volunteers to use tech skills to improve their communities
- [Community Legal Services in East Palo Alto](http://clsepa.org/) - Procides legal aid to low income tenants in East Palo
- [TechEquity](https://techequitycollaborative.org/) - TechEquity Collaborative is a non-profit organization that believes the tech industry can and should contribute to broad-based growth that benefits everyone. TechEquity was a co-sponsor of the Tenant Protection Act of 2019 (AB 1482).
